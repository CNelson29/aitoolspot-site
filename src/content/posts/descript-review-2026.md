## What is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered audio and video editing platform that lets you edit media the same way you edit a text document. Instead of wrestling with traditional timelines, you simply read and edit the auto-generated transcript — delete a word in the transcript and the corresponding audio or video clip disappears instantly. Launched in 2017 and continuously updated through 2026, Descript has become a go-to tool for podcasters, YouTubers, marketers, and remote teams who need to produce polished content without a steep learning curve.

At its core, Descript combines transcription, screen recording, multitrack editing, and AI-driven features like voice cloning, filler-word removal, and AI eye contact correction into a single desktop and web application. The 2026 version introduces several meaningful upgrades, including a revamped AI Studio Sound engine, faster cloud rendering, and deeper integration with popular platforms like YouTube, Spotify, and LinkedIn.

## Key Features

### Text-Based Editing

Descript's headline feature remains its text-based editing engine. Every recording is automatically transcribed with impressive accuracy — typically 95 %+ for clear English speech — and every edit you make to the transcript is reflected in the timeline in real time. This dramatically lowers the barrier for non-editors and speeds up rough-cut creation for professionals.

### Overdub (AI Voice Cloning)

Overdub lets you create a synthetic clone of your own voice. Type a correction and Descript generates audio that sounds like you recorded it. In 2026 the model has been retrained on larger datasets, producing noticeably more natural prosody and fewer robotic artefacts. It is strictly limited to your own voice after a consent-based training process, keeping ethical guardrails in place.

### Studio Sound & Noise Removal

The AI Studio Sound filter removes background noise, room reverb, and mic hiss in a single click. The 2026 engine handles challenging environments — coffee shops, home offices with HVAC noise — far better than the 2024 version, rivalling dedicated tools like Adobe Podcast Enhance.

### Filler Word Removal

Descript automatically detects and highlights filler words such as 'um', 'uh', 'like', and 'you know'. You can remove all instances in bulk or review each one individually. This alone saves hours on long-form podcast episodes.

### AI Eye Contact Correction

A subtle but powerful feature: Descript's eye contact correction uses computer vision to redirect the speaker's gaze toward the camera even when they are reading notes off-screen. Results are convincing for most faces, though extreme head angles can still produce minor artefacts.

### Screen Recording & Composition

Built-in screen recording with simultaneous webcam capture makes Descript a credible alternative to tools like Loom for tutorial and demo content. The 2026 Composition editor adds animated lower-thirds, customisable templates, and a brand kit feature for consistent visual identity across episodes.

### Collaboration

Cloud-based projects allow multiple team members to comment, suggest edits, and publish simultaneously. Version history and role-based permissions make it suitable for small production teams and agencies.

### Publishing & Distribution

One-click publishing to YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, LinkedIn, and TikTok is available on paid plans. Automatic chapter markers, show notes generation, and social clip creation (powered by AI highlight detection) round out the distribution workflow.

Descript is a remarkable tool, but it is not the right fit for everyone. You should look elsewhere if:

Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve remain the industry standard for that work.- **Your primary language is not English:** While Descript supports over 20 languages for transcription, the AI enhancement features — Overdub, filler word detection, AI show notes — are optimised for English. ## FAQ

### Is Descript good for beginners in 2026?

Yes. Descript is one of the most beginner-friendly video and audio editors available. The text-based editing model means you do not need to learn a traditional timeline, and the AI features handle technical tasks like noise removal automatically. Most new users are editing their first project within 30 minutes of signing up.

### How accurate is Descript's transcription?

For clear English speech, Descript consistently achieves 95 %+ accuracy in 2026. Accuracy decreases with strong accents, heavy background noise, or technical jargon. You can always manually correct the transcript, and corrections are reflected instantly in the edit.

### Can I use Descript for free?

Yes, there is a permanent free tier that includes 1 hour of transcription per month and basic editing with watermarked exports. For serious content creation, the paid plans starting at $19/month are necessary. A 14-day free trial of the Creator plan is available without a credit card.

### Does Descript work on Windows and Mac?

Yes. Descript has native desktop applications for both macOS and Windows, as well as a web-based editor for browser access. The desktop apps offer the best performance, particularly for video projects.

### Is Overdub safe and ethical?

Descript requires you to record your own voice and provide explicit consent before training an Overdub model. The feature is locked to your own voice and cannot be used to clone other people's voices without their consent. Descript's terms of service prohibit misuse, and the company has built-in safeguards to prevent abuse.

### How does Descript compare to Adobe Premiere Pro?

They serve different use cases. Descript excels at fast, AI-assisted editing for podcasts, interviews, tutorials, and social content. Adobe Premiere Pro is a full professional NLE with superior colour grading, effects, and compositing capabilities. Many professional creators use both: Descript for rough cuts and AI cleanup, Premiere Pro for final polish on high-production projects.

### What file formats does Descript support?

Descript supports all major audio formats (MP3, WAV, AAC, FLAC) and video formats (MP4, MOV, MKV, WebM). Export options include MP4, MP3, WAV, and SRT subtitle files. 4K export is available on Creator and Business plans.
