## What is Khanmigo?

Launched to the public and refined through 2025 into 2026, Khanmigo acts as a Socratic guide rather than a simple answer machine — nudging students to think critically instead of handing them solutions on a silver platter.

Unlike generic AI chatbots, Khanmigo is deeply integrated into Khan Academy's vast library of math, science, humanities, and test-prep content. It knows where a student is in their learning journey and tailors its responses accordingly. Whether you're a 4th grader struggling with fractions or a high schooler prepping for the SAT, Khanmigo meets you where you are.

In 2026, Khanmigo has expanded its capabilities significantly, adding stronger writing coaching tools, improved teacher dashboards, and more nuanced conversation memory — making it one of the most purpose-built AI tutors available today.

## FAQ

### Is Khanmigo free in 2026?

Khanmigo offers a limited free tier with capped monthly interactions. For unlimited access, the Learner Plan costs $9/month or $44/year. Teachers with verified educator accounts can access it for free through Khan Academy.

### What age group is Khanmigo best suited for?

Khanmigo is designed primarily for K-12 students (ages 6–18), though it can also support adult learners revisiting foundational subjects or preparing for standardized tests like the SAT or GED.

### Does Khanmigo give direct answers to homework questions?

No — and that's intentional. Khanmigo uses the Socratic method, asking guiding questions to help students work through problems themselves. This builds genuine understanding rather than enabling academic shortcuts.

### How does Khanmigo compare to ChatGPT for studying?

ChatGPT is more flexible and powerful for a wider range of tasks, but it lacks Khanmigo's educational guardrails, curriculum integration, and child-safety features. For K-12 learning specifically, Khanmigo is the more appropriate and effective choice.

### Is Khanmigo safe for children?

Yes. Khanmigo is built with strict content filters, age-appropriate language, and child-safety protocols. It's one of the safest AI tools available for young learners, backed by Khan Academy's nonprofit mission.

### Can teachers use Khanmigo in the classroom?

Absolutely. Khanmigo includes a dedicated teacher dashboard for monitoring student progress, identifying learning gaps, and facilitating classroom use. Verified educators can access it for free through Khan Academy for Teachers.
