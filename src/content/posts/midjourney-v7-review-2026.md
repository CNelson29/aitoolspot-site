## What is Midjourney?

Midjourney is a subscription-based text-to-image AI that has dominated the visual quality crown since V5. Version 7, released in early 2026, is the most significant upgrade in its history — bringing near-photorealistic rendering, a trained personalization engine, and dramatically improved prompt fidelity. It runs entirely on Midjourney's cloud infrastructure via Discord and a web dashboard at midjourney.com.

## Key Features of Midjourney V7

- **Photorealism Mode** — A dedicated rendering pipeline that closes the gap with professional photography on controlled scenes and portraits.
- **Personalization Engine (--p flag)** — After rating 200+ images, V7 learns your aesthetic and automatically skews all output toward your taste. It genuinely works.
- **Stronger Prompt Adherence** — V7 dramatically reduces hallucinated elements. Multi-subject and compositionally complex prompts finally land as described.
- **Character and Style References (--cref / --sref)** — Maintain a consistent character face or visual style across an entire series of images without additional prompting.
- **Draft Mode** — 10x faster, low-cost generation for rapid concept iteration before committing GPU time to full-quality renders.
- **Vary Region (Inpainting)** — Edit specific areas of any generated image without regenerating the full composition.
- **Web Dashboard** — Organized galleries, project folders, and batch prompting now make midjourney.com a viable alternative to Discord for day-to-day work.

## Who Should NOT Use Midjourney

- **Budget-conscious beginners** — FLUX.1 Dev (free via Replicate) or Adobe Firefly free tier let you experiment without a subscription commitment.
- **Developers who need an API** — Midjourney has no public API in 2026. Use Replicate-hosted FLUX.1 or Stability AI's API instead.
- **Users who need legible text in images** — DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT handles in-image typography far more reliably than V7.
- **Privacy-sensitive client work on a budget** — Your images are publicly visible on Basic and Standard plans. Stealth Mode costs $60/mo minimum.
- **Teams needing bulk generation** — GPU hour caps hit hard at scale. Commercial API alternatives offer more predictable per-image pricing.
