## What is Khanmigo?

Khanmigo is an AI-powered tutoring assistant developed by **Khan Academy**, built on top of OpenAI's GPT-4 technology. Launched to the public and refined through 2025 into 2026, Khanmigo acts as a Socratic guide rather than a simple answer machine — nudging students to think critically instead of handing them solutions on a silver platter.

Unlike generic AI chatbots, Khanmigo is deeply integrated into the Khan Academy ecosystem, meaning it can reference specific lessons, exercises, and grade-level content in real time. Whether you're a K-12 student struggling with algebra, a parent looking for homework support, or a teacher wanting a classroom co-pilot, Khanmigo positions itself as a safe, educationally sound AI companion.
