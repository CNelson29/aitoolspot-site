## What is Synthesia?

Synthesia is a browser-based AI video platform that lets you create professional presenter-style videos using AI avatars and text-to-speech narration — no camera, studio, or acting required. You write a script, choose an avatar, pick a template, and Synthesia renders a polished talking-head video in minutes.

Launched in 2017 and now serving over 50,000 companies including Google, Nike, and Reuters, Synthesia has become the default choice for corporate training, onboarding, and multilingual marketing video in 2026.

## Key Features

- **160+ AI Avatars** — Diverse, realistic presenters across ethnicities, ages, and styles. Custom avatar creation is available on paid plans.
- **130+ Languages and Accents** — Automatic text-to-speech with natural intonation and accurate lip-sync in virtually every major language.
- **1-Click Translation** — Duplicate any video into another language in seconds; the avatar lip-syncs automatically to the new audio.
- **Template Library** — 60+ pre-built layouts for training, sales, onboarding, and explainer content.
- **Screen Recorder** — Capture your screen and blend it with AI avatar narration on a single timeline.
- **Brand Kit** — Upload logos, colors, and fonts; Synthesia applies them consistently across every video.
- **Collaboration Tools** — Multi-user workspaces with commenting, versioning, and role-based access control.
- **LMS and API Integrations** — Native embeds for Docebo, TalentLMS, and others; REST API access on Enterprise.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Fastest path from script to finished video — under 10 minutes for a standard explainer
- Convincing AI avatars with accurate multilingual lip-sync in 2026
- 1-click translation is a massive time-saver for global teams
- No video editing experience required whatsoever
- Strong brand consistency controls across all videos
- SOC 2 Type II certified — enterprise-grade data security

### Cons

- Avatars still carry a faint uncanny-valley quality on large screens
- No timeline-level audio mixing — can't layer background music precisely
- Video export capped at 1080p; no 4K option
- Starter plan's 10-video monthly limit is restrictive for active teams
- Custom avatar creation adds cost and a 1–2 day review delay

## Who Should NOT Use Synthesia

- **Filmmakers or creative directors** who need full cinematic control — Synthesia is a template-driven tool, not a production suite.
- **Brands requiring 4K or broadcast-quality output** — the 1080p ceiling is a hard limit in 2026.
- **Budget-conscious creators making fewer than 3 videos per month** — the cost-per-video math at the Starter tier rarely makes sense below that threshold.
- **Anyone selling on personal authenticity** — for founder-led brand content or high-trust sales videos, a real human face still outperforms any AI avatar.
