## What is Socratic by Google?

Socratic by Google is a free AI-powered learning app designed to help students from middle school through college understand their homework and coursework. Developed and maintained by Google, Socratic uses advanced AI, optical character recognition (OCR), and Google Search technology to break down complex problems into easy-to-understand explanations.

Students can snap a photo of a question, type it in, or speak it aloud. Socratic then returns curated explanations, videos, step-by-step guides, and visual aids sourced from trusted educational platforms. It covers subjects including Math, Science, History, English Literature, and more.

In 2026, Socratic remains one of the most widely used free AI tutoring tools globally, particularly popular among high school students who need quick, reliable homework help without a subscription fee.

## FAQ

### Is Socratic by Google still available in 2026?

Yes, Socratic by Google is still fully available and actively maintained in 2026. It remains free to download on iOS and Android devices.

### Is Socratic better than Khanmigo for math?

For quick math homework help, Socratic is excellent — especially with its photo input feature. However, Khanmigo provides a more guided, step-by-step conversational approach that helps students truly understand mathematical concepts rather than just getting answers.

### Does Socratic by Google use ChatGPT?

No. Socratic uses Google's own AI models and search infrastructure, not OpenAI's ChatGPT or GPT-4. Khanmigo, by contrast, is powered by GPT-4.

### Can teachers use Socratic by Google?

Socratic is designed for students, not teachers. It has no classroom management, assignment creation, or student progress tracking features. Teachers looking for AI tools should explore Khanmigo for Teachers or Google Classroom integrations.

### Is Socratic by Google safe for kids?

Yes. Socratic is built with Google's SafeSearch technology and is designed to be safe and appropriate for students of all ages, including younger children.

### What subjects does Socratic cover?

Socratic covers a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, History, English Literature, Social Studies, and more. It is most effective for high school-level content.

### Does Socratic give you the answers directly?

Socratic provides explanations, step-by-step solutions, and educational resources rather than just raw answers. The goal is to help students understand the material, though some critics argue it can make it too easy to skip the learning process.
